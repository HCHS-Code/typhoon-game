How To Play
THE BOMB/TYPHOON GAME

Varying on the size of the group, this game can take 20-40~min to conclude.

Basic game loop goes as follows:

The game board is a grid of 5 by 5 with the letters A-E along the left side and 1-5 along the top. Each box of the grid has a question mark, a bomb, a typhoon, poison, or a red cross. Split your group into teams (2-4 teams works best). Teams take turns choosing a box on the grid (e.g. “A2”), and the group leader clicks on the box to reveal the symbol

Question mark

Students must answer a question. They can get a point for a correct answer.

Bomb

Subtract 1 point from your teams total

Typhoon

Wipe your teams points to 0

Poison

Prevent someone from the enemy team(s) from answering questions

Red Cross

Revive a "Poisoned" peroson from your team so they may answer questions again
